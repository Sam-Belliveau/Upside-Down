
	-----= How To Make Levels =-----

	Image rules:
		a. Must be a bmp
		b. Must be 24x256
		c. Names must follow L[Number].bmp, levels will be played in order

	Making Levels:
		a. It is suggested to put the level number in the top left in lava.
		b. The colors (Each color chanel is detected once above 128)
			Red = Lava
			Green = Land
			Blue = Bounce (Changes gravity)
		c. The end of a level is automatically the end.
